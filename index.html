Who am I ? Well! I am an ever-developing web developer and graphic designer, skilled to one degree or another in several relevant areas (and a few irrevelant ones, too). Throughout my time I have helped develop and design websites for many startups and small businesses:
  • a Thai restaurant
  • a Waldorf school
  • ZebraMinds; an interactive children's book app startup company
  • an Americana singer-songwriter
  • a vintage costume shop
  • a sex addicts anonymous group
  • and of course Tom the Book Guy, where I worked for nearly seven years.
For some of these businesses I also had the opportunity to design logos, album art, flyers, fonts, and even a big city billboard campaign featuring Victorian post mortem photography.
❦
Relevant skills and such:
  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
    • I have been working with HTML and CSS for over twenty years, to one extent or another. Javascript is a more recent development that I am excited to explore further. From coding in Notepad and uploading my fansites through FTP to Tripod and FortuneCity, to Wordpress, and finally finding my way to React.
  • React, SQL, Express, Node.js
    • Similarly with Javascript, my experience with these is much more recent. I am especially enamored with React, and all its inherent possibilities.
  • Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop
  • Social Media Management
    • I have always been fascinated by the usage and evolution of blogging platforms and social media. Most notably, I ran the website and social media for Tom The Book Guy for seven years, utilizing Facebook, Twitter, and Pinterest. For a short while I ran the social media for a costume shop in Denver, where (among other things) I got to design a billboard campaign.
  • Wordpress
  • Typography
    • A fascination and side obsession, that plays nicely into all my design projects, especially in book design and my Curiouser House Publishing. I've made a couple of fonts:
      • FriedrichWilhelm

My passion in life is, broadly speaking, to design and create beautiful, useful things; aesthetically pleasing, viscerally satisfying, graphically clean and cohesive, nutrient-rich product, whatever medium it may find itself in.
❦
The internet, and website design specifically, have thus fascinated me since the moment my family got a computer. I have tinkered away ever since, with HTML, CSS, and recently diving into JavaScript and other such things. I have seen the rise and fall of many a trend, many a service. Websites have infinite design potential. Terrifying, and exhilarating at the same time.
